Tips for best matches

  • For DOCX, use built-in highlight colors rather than custom shading for the cleanest matches.
  • For PDF, ensure highlights are actual annotations (not just colored text) so they can be detected.
  • For PPTX, text boxes with highlight fills are captured; background-only fills may be skipped.

All processing is in-browser and temporary.
